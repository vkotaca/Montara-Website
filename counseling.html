Our Process

Five Phases to Admissions Success

A proven 4–6 month process that takes your student from self-discovery to submission day with confidence.

🔍
Month 1 – 1.5

Foundation

We start by deeply understanding your student — who they are, what they’ve experienced, and where the gaps are.

Parent Interview30-minute session to understand family background and key experiences from your student’s life
Student Discovery Sessions2–3 live sessions (45–60 min each) including a timeline project where students map their 10 most significant life events
Resume & Gap AnalysisQuest reviews transcripts, the student’s EC list, and identifies holes in their profile
Profile EnhancementSpecific project recommendations to strengthen candidacy, with opportunities through the Ascend ecosystem
School Priority ListA strategic list built around your family’s priorities, with time allocated accordingly
✍️
Month 2 – 2.5

Writing Development

Your student begins working with our Stanford writing specialist in weekly live sessions plus async review.

Storytelling AssessmentWe review the timeline project writing to identify which stories your student tells powerfully and which need development
Voice DevelopmentLive sessions focused on finding and refining your student’s authentic written voice
Narrative FoundationsBuilding the raw material that will fuel every essay across every application
🎯
2 – 3 Weeks

Essay Strategy

Quest takes the lead on the critical “middle section” — connecting your student’s stories to each school’s specific prompts.

Prompt BreakdownMultiple strategy calls with Quest to analyze every school’s essay prompts
Story-to-Prompt MappingMatching your student’s strongest narratives to the prompts where they’ll have the most impact
Outlines & StrategyDetailed essay outlines and a comprehensive narrative strategy for each target school
📝
1 – 3 Months

Essay Writing Cycle

Students turn outlines into polished essays through an intensive, multi-round writing process.

Weekly Writing Sessions1-hour live sessions with our writing specialist to develop and refine each essay

EC List & Activities SectionCrafting a compelling extracurricular narrative
Recommendation StrategyGuidance on selecting and positioning letters of recommendation

Co-Founder and Head of College Counseling

Meet Quest

Quest is the founder and lead counselor at Montara College Counseling. His approach is built on three convictions: that every student has a compelling story to tell, that strategic profile-building matters as much as polished essays, and that time should be spent where it has the greatest impact — on your student’s highest-priority schools.

With acceptances to Stanford, Harvard, Yale, MIT, and dozens of other top universities, Quest brings both a proven track record and an intensely personal approach. He works directly with each family to build strategy, develop narrative, and ensure every piece of the application works together.

Quest is supported by a Stanford undergraduate writing specialist who meets with students weekly — providing the dedicated, hands-on writing support that transforms good essays into exceptional ones.
