What We Offer

Test Prep Programs

Every student gets a customized approach based on a thorough diagnostic assessment.

SAT Private Tutoring

One-on-one sessions with a top-scoring tutor, fully customized to close your student’s specific gaps. Flexible scheduling, in-person or virtual.

ACT Private Tutoring

Targeted instruction across all four ACT sections. We specialize in science reasoning, time management, and English strategy.

Small Group Courses

Cohorts of 4–6 students covering all test content in a structured 8-week curriculum. Collaborative, rigorous, and more affordable.

Intensive Boot Camps

Weekend and school-break intensives for students who need rapid improvement before an upcoming test date.

Our Process

How It Works

1

Free diagnostic assessment

Your student takes a full-length practice test. We analyze every answer to build a precise picture of strengths, weaknesses, and opportunity areas.

2

Custom study plan

We develop a study roadmap that targets high-impact areas first, tracks progress, and accounts for your student’s schedule and test date.

3

Expert instruction

Sessions are led by instructors who scored in the 99th percentile. They teach strategy, not just content — because test-taking is a skill.

4

Track progress & adjust

We run regular practice tests, share results with parents, and adjust the plan as needed to ensure steady improvement toward the target score.
