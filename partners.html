Our Partners

Montara Education is part of a network of organizations dedicated to developing exceptional students — from competitive debate to academic mentorship.

Ascend Speech & Debate

Elite competitive speech and debate training

Ascend Speech & Debate trains the next generation of world-class speakers, thinkers, and leaders through intensive summer programs and year-round coaching. With programs in California, Florida, and online, Ascend has assembled one of the most elite coaching staffs in the country — national champions, award-winning coaches, and former students now leading the circuit.

  • Congressional Debate, Extemporaneous Speaking & Public Forum
  • Summer intensives at University of the Pacific and Lynn University
  • Online programs for remote access
Visit ascendspeech.org →

Ascend Academy

Year-round debate & public speaking coaching

Ascend Academy provides year-round coaching and programs for high school and middle school students in speech and debate. Open registration throughout the school year, Ascend Academy offers an accessible entry point for students looking to develop their competitive speaking skills and build a meaningful extracurricular profile.

  • Group coaching sessions and prep review
  • Open enrollment for all skill levels
  • Congressional Debate focus with expert mentorship
Visit ascendacademy.org →

Sandel Academy

Application-only elite debate mentorship

Sandel Academy is personally run by Quest Sandel, Co-Founder of Montara Education. The academy offers an intensive, application-only Congressional Debate program with 20 group coaching sessions per month, unlimited prep review, elite one-on-one mentorship, and on-site travel tournament coaching.

  • 20 group coaching sessions per month
  • Unlimited prep review & elite 1:1 mentorship
  • On-site travel tournament coaching
  • Application-only admission
Visit sandelacademy.com →
